Bio

Cao Ljudi,

Hey there! I'm Borka, a UX/UI designer with a touch of magic and a lot of humor. I was born in a country that no longer exists, and I left war-torn Yugoslavia in pursuit of my American Dream. Talk about taking design to new dimensions!

My design journey began with studying urban geography and wandering through cities. I'm a fan of William "Holy" Whyte and his mantra: "You have to get out and walk!" After all, everything looks different from a human perspective, especially when you're on a flying carpet bridging two worlds. I've got a background in landscape architecture too, which adds another layer to my design skills. But lately, I've been on fire with UX/UI design, sparking exciting possibilities. I love using human-centered and responsive design principles. It's like completing a cosmic loop where I can unleash my creativity, even while floating on my enchanted carpet. Talk about flexibility!

Design isn't just a job for me; it's a way of life. I bring design principles into everything I do, from self-care to advocating for those with invisible chronic diseases. And with a name that means "female fighter," I continue to "fight like a girl!" Coincidentally, my name also means a pine tree, symbolizing wisdom and longevity. So, I embrace that symbolism and cherish my beloved Aleppo Pine, which brings back wonderful childhood memories.

But hey, design isn't my only passion. I'm a lover and collector of niche perfumes, a fashionista, a concertgoer, and a huge fan of the band Tool! So let's connect, keep rocking, and add some design magic to the mix!
